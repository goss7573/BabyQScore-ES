BabyQscore

BabyQscore.org was created by Mark Gostine, MD, a dedicated father of four, grandfather of six, lifelong runner, and nutritional advocate. After treating chronic disease for much of his professional career, he realized with the birth of his first grandchild that good health truly begins in the womb — providing the inspiration for BabyQscore.org.

The BabyQ Score is built on the recommendations of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) and outcome data from CDC WONDER, based on tens of millions of U.S. deliveries.

Whether you are pregnant now or planning a pregnancy, BabyQ Score helps you learn how to have the healthiest pregnancy possible.
