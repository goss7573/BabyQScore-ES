Pregnancy Complication Risk Results

Our risk groups are based on national data from the CDC WONDER Natality Program. We divide women into 640 finely segmented groups using a combination of factors — including age, BMI, social circumstances, personal habits, geographic region, and access to healthcare. These groups are built from outcomes of tens of millions of births recorded over the most recent eight years.

Some characteristics, such as your age, cannot be changed. Others, like nutrition, activity, and stress management, can be improved — and increasing your BabyQ score helps you focus on the things you can control.
