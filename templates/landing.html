About BabyQ

The BabyQscore was created by Mark Gostine, MD, inspired by the birth of his first grandchild. Through his clinical experience, he came to appreciate that good health truly begins in the womb — a time when a mother’s lifestyle choices can, through epigenetics, turn good genes on and bad genes off, with effects that may shape a child’s health for a lifetime.

The BabyQ Score is built on the recommendations of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) and outcome data from CDC WONDER, based on tens of millions of U.S. deliveries. Whether you are pregnant now or planning a pregnancy, BabyQ Score helps you learn how to have the healthiest pregnancy possible.
