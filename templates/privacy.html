Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

BabyQscore.org LLC (“BabyQscore,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website babyqscore.org or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide – such as your name, email address, year of birth, and responses to the BabyQ wellness questions.
  • Automatically collected information – such as browser type, device type, and general usage data collected through our analytics provider (Plausible.io).

We do not use cookies, or advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your BabyQ Score and display personalized feedback.
  • Improve the BabyQ website and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about updates or your account (if applicable).

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party.

3. Data Security

We use secure (HTTPS) connections and industry-standard encryption to protect your information. Access to any personal data is restricted to authorized personnel involved in maintaining the BabyQ platform.

4. Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused, GDPR-compliant analytics service, to measure website traffic and usage trends. Plausible does not use cookies or collect personally identifiable information.
